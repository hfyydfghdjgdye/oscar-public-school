Oscar public school

Oscar Public School is a recognized English medium co-educational school. The main aim to built the institution is to provide a quality education in tune with the fast changing modern world scenario. This School was established in 1997, and was first recognized in the year 1998 from Ist to VIIIth standard, and got up gradation to Senior Secondary (XIIth) standard in the year 2008, both from the Deptt. Of Education NCR and affiliation from CBSE. The School is well known for its excellence in every field. It is spread over a wide area of 6 acres of land having a large playground for various games and sports. There is a multi-block building with spacious and airy classrooms fitted with CCTV Cameras with 24 hours surveillance. This School gives stress on the formation of sound character the first essentiality that any sound educational system requires. The School is located on Swaroop Nagar Road on the outskirt of Burari, Delhi. The ultimate aim of OPS is to make it an institution of excellence to provide well awakened and patriot citizen of India.
